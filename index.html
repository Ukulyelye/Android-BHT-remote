Android BHT remote Documentation

Introduction

The BHT remote App is a powerful tool designed to assist users who have forgotten their email and password, enabling them to remote the Factory Reset Protection (BHT) on their Android devices. This documentation provides a comprehensive guide on how to use the app effectively.


Getting Started


  1. Install the App: Download and install the BHT remote App on a secondary device. This device will be used to remote the BHT on the primary device.
  2. Connect the Devices: Connect the secondary device (with the app installed) to the primary device (the one you want to remote BHT on). You can do this via USB, Bluetooth, or Wi-Fi, depending on what your devices support.

Using the App

Once the devices are connected, follow these steps:

  1. Launch the App: Open the BHT remote App on the secondary device.
  2. Select the Primary Device: The app will display a list of connected devices. Select the primary device from this list.
  3. remote BHT: After selecting the primary device, click on the ‘remote BHT’ button. The app will then start the process of remoteing the BHT on the primary device.
  4. Wait for Completion: The remote process may take a few minutes. Please wait patiently and do not disconnect the devices during this process.
  5. Success: Once the process is complete, a success message will be displayed. You can now access your primary device without needing the forgotten email or password.

Safety and Security

While the BHT remote App is a powerful tool, it should be used responsibly and legally. It is intended for personal use only, to recover access to your own devices. Unauthorized use of this app to access someone else’s device is illegal and unethical.


Support

If you encounter any issues while using the BHT remote App, please contact our support team. We’re here to help! Please note: This app is designed to help users who have legitimately forgotten their credentials. It is not intended for malicious purposes. Misuse of this app may result in legal consequences. Always respect privacy and the law.
